About H.A.R.C.

The idea

When we first imagined H.A.R.C., one central question guided us: "How can we help?" After a few rounds of brainstorming and refining the idea, H.A.R.C. was born.

Throughout the development process, we kept encountering the same question: "Why?"
Some argued that a resource calculation tool wasn't necessary — after all, they said, you could find information or do the math without needing another website. But funny enough, that argument revealed the very problem we set out to solve.

Yes, H.A.R.C. is a resource you visit — but it's not just another tool; it’s designed to be the standalone solution for resource planning.
Instead of wasting time chasing scattered information across the internet, H.A.R.C. brings everything you need into one streamlined place. During development, we saw firsthand how critical data was often buried under dead links, fragmented across countless sites, or just plain difficult to find.

Our goal is simple: to cut through the noise, save you time, and help you focus on what truly matters — fulfilling your mission and helping those who need it most.
